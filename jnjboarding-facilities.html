Our Boarding


Our school among many services provides for boarding services to learners. We have a plethora of boarding facilities in the boarding section for both girls and boys The Matron, patron are in charge of the boarding section alongside our dedicated boarding teachers who stay in the school with learners. We provide clean and safe bathing water, and hot showers to our pupils in the boarding section. We have enough full board and well maintained and secured boarding facilities.
Apart from normal classes, our learners enjoy entertainment on sartudays and spiritual nourishment for all religions: Be it Christianity, Islamic, Hinduism, among other religions. SDA's attend there services on sartudays whereas i.e Catholics, and Protestants do same on Sundays morning. Incase of emergencies in the school, we have emergency response protocols and in that case of learner falling sick or "emergency sickness" we have First Aid trained staff (Teachers); where the process involves informing the learner's parent or the guardian.
