All About Us

We are a well-established private school that has been offering quality education since 1985. We offer both CBC Curriculum from Kindergarten to Junior Secondary School. Our teachers are passionate, caring and professional. We have modernized, spacious and safe facilities for our students. We continue to making and enhancing confidence in our students. “We are all successful because our children are successful.”

What to Know First

Jack and Jill is a private ECDE, Primary and Junior Secondary level school situated in Kitale - Trans Nzoia County alongside [Eldoret - Kitale] Road. Over the years we have grown to be a global brand with children, pupils and students from all over the world joining to learn basic education. We have proudly over 17000 alumni. We have had pupils who have succeeded and got admissions to various National Schools in the country including recently : Mang'u Boys High; Maranda High School; St Brigid Girls Kiminini and Starehe High Schools.

Our Transportation

The School offers transport to over 80% of the day students at Jack and Jill School. To ease collection and dropping time, the school has segmented the town into 6 routes each of which is serviced by a dedicated bus or van. The routes are Milimani Route which services the Milimani Estate and Shimo La Tewa areas, Gatua Route which services Kwa Muthoni, Gatua, Village inn and Sirende areas, Too Station Route which service St Joseph Tool Station, Wamuini and Mabonde areas, Town Route which caters for town center, Machinjoni, Bondeni, Lessos Section 19 and Police Line, Goseta Route which covers St Mary's up to Goseta and the last is Legacy Route which caters for Kibomet, Legacy and Forest.

  All routes have teachers who take care of pupils.

Our Philosophy

A child's experiences during the preschool years influence later development and outlook toward learning. Jack and Jill School is committed to our goal of helping each child develop a positive self-image. We provide many opportunities for accomplishment, in offering a variety of materials, a safe and appealing environment in which to work and play, and encouragement for the child to proceed at his/her own pace. As the child gains confidence in his/her abilities, he/she will also better the social interactions with peers and the adults in his/her life. The staff is here to facilitate the childs' success in developing creativity, building a healthy self-concept, and acquiring self-direction. We intend to promote a sense of wonder and curiosity, to fuel the childs' enthusiasm for learning about his/her world. We offer the opportunity for individual activities and participation in small groups. We suggest daily outdoor experiences to use large muscles and develop those skills, as well as to learn about and interact with the outdoor environment.
