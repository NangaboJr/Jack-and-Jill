The World Environment
Day !

The World Environment Day is the biggest international day for the environment. This day globally is led by the UNEP : United Nations Environmental Programme and it's held on 5th June annualy since inception of UNEP and this day marks the fisrt day of Stockholm Conference on Human Environment with last year (2023) marking a Golden Jubilee year of UNEP since its designationation by the UN General Assembly. UNEP has grown to be the largest platform for the outreach celebrated by millions across the world.
This year (2024) the host country for the UNEP annual conference which is usually participated by various environmental organizations across the world will be Saudi Arabia with a focus on land restoration, desertification, and drought resilience
Here at our school We' just like other instutions and organizations who are environmental and climate friendly #celebrated the day, #planted new trees and #beatplasticpollution within our compound...
