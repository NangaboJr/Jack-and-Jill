What To Know First

Jack and Jill School is a private school situated in Kitale, Trans Nzoia County. The school provides programmes in Early Child Development Learning or kindargarten, Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) and Junior High level programmes. Our programmes are linked to providing the learners with both physical ability, mental care and academic or rather intellectual knowledge. We have different aftercare/beyond curriculum programmes. Over the years we have grown to be a global brand in providing basic education with over 32000 alumni. To find more about us continue exploring our website.

Our Profile

  • Jack and Jill School - KITALE is a private primary and junior secondary school situated in Kitale; Trans Nzoia County alongside Eldoret - Kitale Road. The school was founded almost a jubilee years ago as the Royal Kindergarten for language mastering and training with the first lot of children into the Early Child Development Learning. Over the years since its inception in 1984 as Jack and Jill School, the school has grown to be a global brand with learners from all over the world joining to learn and understand the Kenyan Basic Education Curriculum. The school has proudly and successfully produced learners with high marks who have acquired admissions to various National Schools in the country including recently: Mang'u Boys High; Maranda High School; St Brigid Girls Kiminini and Starehe High Schools. Jack and Jill School proudly boosts an alumni association of over and about 32000 professional, well educated and global transformational leaders around the continent and abroad

  • Since its inception, the school has continued to grow and develop in terms of academic programmes|aftercare|infrastructure to cater for the increasing demand for basic | aftercare and beyond curriculum activities. Currently, our academic programmes are organized into different sections i.e 1.Science and Technology; icluding computer and ICT programmes. 2.Agriculture and Green Economy 3. Languages and Literature among other sections which include Beyond Curriculum Activities: Chess, Golf, Swimming, Drama, Play and Music ...

  • A global competitive school producing the next generation of excellent, competent and transformational leaders.

  • To provide a safe environment where all students are nurtured to achieve both academic excellence and holistic development; develop confidence, knowledge and skills set to succeed in the global field.
