Our Facilities

The quality of our school facilities and infrastructure make a big impact on a child's learning and development in his/her formative years, essentially building the core foundation of their demonstrable characteristics and competencies as a person.

Computer Lab

Our computer lab serves as a place where students can come to learn different technological applications, such as coding, virtual design or even excel spreadsheets. It also serves as a place where students do their technical homework, research, or work on other online assignments.

Chess Club

Chess Club in our school offer a wide variety of activities. There can be speed chess or rated games, or both. The club activities sometimes include simultaneous exhibitions, lectures, and even formal classes taught by chess Masters and Expert Teachers.

About Us

Jack and Jill is a private ECDE, Primary and Junior Secondary level school situated in Kitale - Trans Nzoia County alongside [Eldoret - Kitale] Road. Over the years we have grown to be a global brand with children, pupils and students from all over the world joining to learn basic education. We have proudly over 17000 alumni. We have had pupils who have succeeded and got admissions to various National Schools in the country including recently : Mang'u Boys High; Maranda High School; St Brigid Girls Kiminini and Starehe High Schools.
